# saas-metrics

Use this skill when calculating, analyzing, or reporting SaaS business metrics. Triggers on MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV, CAC, LTV:CAC ratio, cohort analysis, net revenue retention, expansion revenue, board deck metrics, investor reporting, unit economics, payback period, or SaaS financial modeling. Covers metric definitions, formulas, spreadsheet implementation, cohort tables, and board-ready reporting for founders, finance teams, and growth operators.

## Overview

SaaS metrics are the quantitative language of subscription businesses. They
answer three fundamental questions: how fast is the business growing (MRR, ARR,
net new MRR), how sticky are customers (churn, retention, NRR), and how
efficient is growth (CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC, payback period). This skill equips an
agent to define metrics precisely, calculate them from raw data, build cohort
analyses, and produce board-ready reporting decks that investors and operators
actually trust.
