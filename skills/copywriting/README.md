## Overview

Copywriting is the craft of writing words that persuade people to take action -
buy, sign up, click, subscribe, or believe. Unlike content writing (which informs),
copywriting converts. Every word earns its place. The best copy feels effortless to
read because enormous effort went into writing it. This skill gives an agent the
frameworks, formulas, and judgment to produce headlines, landing pages, CTAs, value
propositions, email subject lines, and product descriptions that actually work.
